Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the BuzzzLogic automation app (“App”) handles your information.

  1. Information Collected: The App does not collect personally identifiable information. It processes only the video files and captions/hashtags you choose to upload to TikTok.
  2. Use of Information: The App uses TikTok’s Content Posting API solely to upload your selected videos to your TikTok account at scheduled times.
  3. Sharing: We do not sell or share your information with third parties. Authentication occurs via OAuth; tokens are used only for uploading.
  4. Security: OAuth credentials are stored securely; sensitive data is not logged.
  5. Changes: Updates to this policy will appear at this URL.

By using the App, you consent to this policy.
